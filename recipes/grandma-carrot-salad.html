Grandma's Carrot Salad

Desciption

This carrot salad is quick and easy to make with minimal ingredients. My grandma made it all the time, and I loved it. Now I make it to honor her, and it's almost as good.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shredded carrots
  • 1 ¼ cups raisins
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  • Step 1: Mix shredded carrots and raisins together in a large serving bowl.
  • Step 2: Whisk mayonnaise, lemon juice, and salt in a small bowl until smooth. Pour over carrot mixture; stir until carrots and raisins are completely coated. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.

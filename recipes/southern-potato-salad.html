Southern potato salad

Desciption

This Southern potato salad includes eggs, celery, and relish and should be served warm.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ stalk celery, chopped
  • ¼ cup sweet relish
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  • Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  • Step 2: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes; drain and chop.
  • Step 3: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes; drain and chop.
  • Step 4: Combine cooked potatoes and eggs with mayonnaise, celery, relish, garlic, mustard, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  • Step 5: Gently mix together and serve warm.
